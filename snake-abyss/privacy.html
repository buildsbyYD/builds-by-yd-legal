Privacy Policy

Snake Abyss: The Infinite Descent

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Snake Abyss: The Infinite Descent. This Privacy Policy explains how Snake Abyss (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding any data processed during your deep-sea descent.

2. Information We Collect

Snake Abyss is designed to be a private, offline-first experience. We do not require you to create a proprietary account or provide personal details such as your name or email address.

  • Local Gameplay Data: Information such as your Pearls, unlocked skins, local high scores, and achievement progress is stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising Data: To provide optional rewarded ads and banner ads, the App uses Google AdMob. Google may collect automatically generated information such as your IP address, device identifiers (including the Android Advertising ID), approximate location information, and ad interaction data. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your device settings, region, and Google’s advertising policies.
  • Leaderboard & Social Data: If you choose to use Google Play Games Services features such as Global Leaderboards or Cloud Achievements, your Google Play Games Gamer ID and leaderboard scores may be transmitted to Google’s servers.
  • Daily Depth Seeds: Daily Depth layouts are generated locally on your device based on the system date. No personal data is transmitted to retrieve these layouts.
  • Local Notifications: To help you protect your daily streak, the App can schedule optional local reminders. These notifications are scheduled and executed entirely on your device using built-in Android background systems. No remote push tokens are collected, and no notification data leaves your device.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • On-Device Storage: Most gameplay data (Pearls, settings, skins, and local progress) remains stored on your device using Android storage systems. We (the developer) cannot access this data.
  • Google Play Games Services: If signed in, certain progress and achievement data may sync through Google Play Games Services and are governed by Google’s own privacy practices.
  • Encryption: Any data transmitted through third-party services (such as leaderboard scores, ad requests, or in-app purchase verification) is sent over secure encrypted connections (HTTPS).

4. Third-Party Services

Snake Abyss integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may process device identifiers and related information to provide ads. Learn more here: How Google uses information from apps and sites
  • Google Play Billing: Used for secure in-app purchases such as ad removal purchases. We do not collect, process, or store payment or financial information. All transactions are securely handled by Google.
  • Google Play Games Services: Used for global rankings, achievements, and optional cloud syncing features. Your Gamer ID and related scores are managed within the Google Play ecosystem according to your Google account privacy settings.

5. Internet Access

Snake Abyss functions primarily offline. Internet access may be used solely for:

  • Loading and displaying advertisements
  • Verifying and processing in-app purchases
  • Displaying and updating leaderboard rankings
  • Unlocking and syncing achievements through Google Play Games Services

6. App Permissions

Snake Abyss operates using a minimal-permissions approach. Depending on your Android version, the App may request the following optional runtime permission:

  • Notifications (Android 13+): Requested strictly to display local scheduled alerts for Daily Depth resets and streak protections.

The App does not request access to your contacts, precise location, camera, microphone, or external file storage.

7. Children’s Privacy

Snake Abyss does not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Leaderboards identify players only through their Google Play Gamer ID as managed by Google Play Games Services.

8. Data Deletion & Control

  • Local Data: You can permanently remove all local game data by clearing the App’s data in your Android settings or by uninstalling the App.
  • Advertising Preferences: You may manage advertising settings through your device settings by adjusting your Google advertising preferences or resetting your Advertising ID.
  • Google Play Games Data: You can manage or remove your Google Play Games profile and related cloud data through the Google Play Games app or your Google account settings.
